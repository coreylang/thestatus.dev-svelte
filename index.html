About The Status

The Status is both a sleek, real‑time User Interface and a powerful backend API designed to keep your customers and staff perfectly in sync.

On the front end, The Status provides a clean, intuitive interface that displays live order updates — from “Preparing” to “Ready” — so customers know exactly when to pick up. It’s designed for clarity, speed, and a touch of personality that keeps guests engaged.

Behind the scenes, our backend API integrates seamlessly with your existing POS or restaurant order management system. This means you can keep your current workflow while adding real‑time visibility for your customers — no extra manual steps, no duplicate data entry.

Whether you run a single café or a multi‑location restaurant group, The Status bridges the gap between your kitchen and your customers, delivering a smoother, more transparent dining experience.
